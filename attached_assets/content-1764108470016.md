## got questions? we've got answers.

Who can join MTC?

+

High schoolers, university students, founders, investors, and both younger and older professionals in the tech industry. We're a community for the tech industry.

What makes MTC different?

+

We focus on shipping over networking. Build real products, learn AI tools, create tangible outcomes. We're raising Muslim makers who put good into the world.

How do I get involved?

+

Students: check our university page for local chapters or email ali@mtc.so to start one. Professionals: join build nights or mentor. Follow @muslimtechcollab for updates.
